Privacy Policy - Steppe Runner

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Steppe Runner ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile game Steppe Runner ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Game scores, gameplay statistics, and session duration.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads.
  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we collect your basic profile information (name and email address) for leaderboard functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To maintain leaderboard and game statistics
  • To analyze app performance and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob's privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase: For authentication and database services. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Google AdMob for advertising purposes
  • Google Firebase for app functionality
  • Service providers who assist in operating our app

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Advertising

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. You can opt out of personalized ads by:

  • Going to your device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Or visiting https://www.google.com/settings/ads

9. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
